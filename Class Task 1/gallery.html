Welcome to The Aurelian Gallery!

The Aurelian Gallery is a platform that celebrates the beauty and diversity of art. Our mission is to inspire and connect artists and art lovers from around the world. Whether you are an artist looking to showcase your work or an art enthusiast seeking inspiration, The Aurelian Gallery is the place for you. Explore our gallery, connect with fellow artists, and discover the beauty of art in all its forms.

Picasso: As a malefic figure who changed the contours of modern art, the name of Pablo Picasso is cloaked in layers of intrigue, brilliance, and verve. Commencing his journey in sunny Spain, Picasso's artistic odyssey saw the dovetailing of various influences and stages, resulting in an art style that remains unmatched in its originality and dynamism. This survey of Picasso's art style takes us through a captivating chronicle of his progression, examining the milestones of his career from his Blue and Rose periods, his instrumental role in the evolution of cubism, the indelible imprints of the World War years on his work, to his innovative techniques, and finally, offering an examination of his profound legacy.
